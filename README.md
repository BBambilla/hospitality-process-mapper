## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click "Add New Student"
3. Select an industry (Restaurant, Hotel, or Theme Park)
4. Enter company name
5. Add 3-5 customer reviews for each process step
6. View process map with risk levels
7. Export your report as PDF or Word

## Industries

- **Fine Dining Restaurant** - 9 process steps
- **International Hotel Chain** - 8 process steps  
- **Recreational Theme Park** - 8 process steps

## Risk Levels

- **LOW RISK:** 0-2 reviews per step
- **MEDIUM RISK:** 3-4 reviews per step
- **HIGH RISK:** 5+ reviews per step
